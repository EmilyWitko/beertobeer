Process:
  1. In brewpot, heat 2 gallons of water to 165° F. Turn off heat.
  2. Steep malts in grain bag for 1/2 hour in brewpot.
  3. Gently drain grain bag into brewpot to get as much liquid out as you can. Do not squeeze bag.
  4. Add 2 gallons of water to brewport and bring to a boil.
  5. Turn off heat and add #1 MALT to brewpot and stir.
